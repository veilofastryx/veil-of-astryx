About

Do you want to join a clan but don’t have the time or patience for all the usual pressure that comes with one? If strict schedules, mandatory scrims, endless tournaments, or awkward real-life meetups aren’t your thing, then Veil of Astryx is exactly what you’ve been looking for. We’re a chill, casual, globally connected community made for people who just want to relax, play when they can, and chat with others from around the world without any commitments. Whether you’re busy with school, work, family, or just life, you’re welcome here — no requirements, no expectations, no stress. You can hang out in chat, share memes, talk about games or anything else, and come and go as you please. If you’ve been searching for a clan where you don’t have to scrim, don’t have to join tournaments, don’t have to participate in real-life meetups, and don’t have to be online every day, this is the place for you. If you want to join a clan that’s genuinely chill, pressure-free, and built for players who just want good vibes and good company, welcome to the family.
